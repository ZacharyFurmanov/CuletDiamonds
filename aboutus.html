About Culet International Corporation

Trusted in the global diamond trade since 1979.

Founded in 1979 by Mr. David Hazghiyan, Culet International Corporation has long been recognized as a trusted leader in the global diamond trade. From our home in New York’s Diamond District, we’ve built a reputation as the premier destination for polished diamonds, earning the confidence of manufacturers, wholesalers, and jewelers worldwide.

As a family-owned business, we hold firm to the values of integrity, service, and partnership. Mr. Hazghiyan and his son Jonathan believe that “You must buy well to sell well.” That philosophy guides our commitment to delivering not only the finest diamonds, but also the most competitive pricing—ensuring our customers’ success. When you work with Culet, you’re not just a client; you’re part of our family.

Over the decades, we have supplied countless jewelry manufacturers and wholesalers with premium polished diamonds. Building on this foundation, we’ve now harnessed our manufacturing resources and decades of expertise to create exceptional fine jewelry collections. This natural evolution allows us to pair world-class craftsmanship with unmatched value, offering our clients both loose diamonds and finished jewelry at the industry’s most competitive prices.

Our advanced manufacturing facilities blend cutting-edge technology with old-world artistry, producing diamonds and jewelry of unparalleled cut, clarity, color, and brilliance. Every stone and every piece is sourced exclusively from authorized, conflict-free suppliers in established diamond-producing countries, reflecting our commitment to ethical business practices.

We welcome you to visit our New York City headquarters or connect with one of our international offices, where our team is ready to provide personalized, family-style service and ensure that your experience with Culet International Corporation exceeds expectations.
